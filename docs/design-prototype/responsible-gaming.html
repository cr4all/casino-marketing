1. Our Commitment

Ascendra Platforms believes iGaming operators have a duty to protect players. Our platform includes responsible gaming (RG) tools that operators can configure and enforce across casino and sportsbook products.

This page describes platform capabilities. Player-facing responsible gaming policies are published by each licensed operator on their own branded websites.

2. Platform Tools for Operators

Ascendra Platforms provides operators with the following player protection features:

  • Deposit limits — daily, weekly, and monthly caps set by players or enforced by operators.
  • Loss and wagering limits — controls on maximum losses or bet amounts over defined periods.
  • Session limits — reminders and automatic logouts after configured play duration.
  • Cooling-off periods — temporary account restrictions chosen by the player.
  • Self-exclusion — extended account closure for players who need a break from gambling.
  • Reality checks — periodic notifications showing time spent and net position.
  • Underage prevention — age verification integrated with KYC workflows.
  • Sportsbook limits — market-level and player-level betting caps.

3. Operator Admin Controls

Operators manage responsible gaming settings through the Ascendra Platforms backoffice — applying limits per player, segment, or jurisdiction, and reviewing accounts flagged for problematic behavior through integrated risk and fraud monitoring.

4. Player Support

Operators can deliver in-platform support via live chat and ticketing, directing players to professional help organizations where appropriate. Ascendra Platforms enables operators to display responsible gaming information and helpline links on player-facing sites.

5. Training and Best Practices

We recommend operators train support and compliance teams on responsible gaming procedures, monitor player behavior indicators, and align RG policies with requirements in each licensed market.

6. Get Help

If you are a player seeking support, please contact the operator of the website where you play. If you are an operator evaluating Ascendra Platforms responsible gaming capabilities, request a demo or email compliance@ascendraplatforms.com.
